About IRCTC

Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation — connecting 1.4 billion Indians through the world's largest rail network since 1999.

🎯
Our Mission

To provide world-class, seamless, and secure railway ticketing and catering services to every Indian traveller. We are committed to leveraging technology to make train travel easier, affordable, and delightful for all citizens.

🔭
Our Vision

To become India's most trusted digital travel platform — from booking to boarding — by integrating technology, innovation, and customer-centricity. We envision a future where every Indian can plan and book their entire journey end-to-end on a single platform.
